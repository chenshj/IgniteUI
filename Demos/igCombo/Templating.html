Templating

There are three places to use custom templates points in the igCombo control: header, footer, and item. The header template appears above the dropdown list and the footer displays below the list. Item templates are repeated for each item within the igCombo control. This sample shows how header and footer templates are used to provide more information about the list as a whole and how the item templates can provide more information about each individual item. This sample accesses the Netflix OData API.
