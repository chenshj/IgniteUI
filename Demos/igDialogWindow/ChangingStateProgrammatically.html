igDialogWindow Changing State Programmatically

This sample demonstrates how to change the states of the igDialog control programmatically. When you click on the create button, the dialog initializes with default content and includes close button. When you click on the Close button the dialog disappears and may be reopened anytime by clicking on the open button. Clicking on the Destroy button completely removes the dialog window from the DOM.
