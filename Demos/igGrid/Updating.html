Updating

This sample demonstrates updating capabilities of igGrid with default options of igGridUpdating.
The selection of igGrid is enabled too. It means that active row will match with editing-row and if grid has focus, then editing of active row can be started from keyboard by F2 or Enter keys, and selected rows can be deleted by the Delete key.
