Virtualization

This sample demonstrates the unique HTML row virtualization capabilities of the igGrid control. Virtualization is valuable when displaying large sets of records to end users. For example, a virtualized grid can support a data source of thousands of records and still provide a responsive experience to the end-user during a rapid scroll of the grid. This responsiveness is present when virtual scrolling and paging are not enabled.
